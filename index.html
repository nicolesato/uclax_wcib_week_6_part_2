Our Story

We, Kristen and Nicole (the og Boba Gals), have a love affair with all things boba and milk tea. In 2018, we decided to travel the world to taste boba from all corners of the earth. Everywhere we went, we learned something new - like the nuances of brewing tea or how to make the perfect boba texture. In 2019, we decided we had to have our own shop: a place where boba queens from near and far could gather to chat and sip. And thus, Boba Gals was born! Now, we are so glad to share it with you.
